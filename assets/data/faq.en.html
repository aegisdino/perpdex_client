4. How are the odds calculated?

The odds automatically fluctuate based on the total amount of gold collected on Up and Down.

Calculation method:

  1. Total gold collected on Up and Down is summed.
  2. A set rake (@@rake@@%) is deducted.
  3. The remaining amount is distributed according to the ratio of betting amounts on Up/Down.
