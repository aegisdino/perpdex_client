Browser and Telegram MiniApp Cache Clearing Guide

Web Browser Cache Clearing

1. How to clear cache in Chrome browser
PC
  1. Click on the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  2. Select "Settings".
  3. Click on "Privacy and security".
  4. Select "Clear browsing data".
  5. Set the time range to "All time".
  6. Check "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files".
  7. Click the "Clear data" button.

Quick method: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete to directly open the clear browsing data window.

Mobile
  1. Open the Chrome app.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap "Settings".
  4. Tap "Privacy and security".
  5. Tap "Clear browsing data".
  6. Set the time range to "All time".
  7. Check "Cookies and site data" and "Cached images and files".
  8. Tap the "Clear data" button.
2. How to clear cache in Safari browser
PC (Mac)
  1. Open Safari browser.
  2. Click "Safari" in the top menu bar.
  3. Select "Settings".
  4. Click the "Privacy" tab.
  5. Click "Manage Website Data".
  6. Click "Remove All" or select specific sites to remove.
  7. Click "Done".

Alternatively, you can select "Safari" > "Clear History and Website Data" for a quicker method.

Mobile (iOS)
  1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Safari".
  3. Tap "Clear History and Website Data".
  4. Tap "Clear History and Data" in the confirmation message.
3. How to clear cache in Firefox browser
PC
  1. Click the menu button (≡) in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Settings".
  3. Click "Privacy & Security".
  4. In the "Cookies and Site Data" section, click "Clear Data".
  5. Check "Cached Web Content" and "Cookies and Site Data".
  6. Click the "Clear" button.

Quick method: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete to directly open the clearing window.

Mobile
  1. Open the Firefox app.
  2. Tap the menu button (≡) in the bottom-right corner.
  3. Tap "Settings".
  4. Tap "Data Management".
  5. Select the data types you want to delete (cache, cookies, etc.).
  6. Tap "Clear Data".
4. How to clear cache in Edge browser
PC
  1. Click the three dots (⋯) in the top-right corner.
  2. Select "Settings".
  3. Click "Privacy, search, and services".
  4. Click "Choose what to clear" under "Clear browsing data".
  5. Set the time range to "All time".
  6. Check "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files".
  7. Click the "Clear now" button.

Quick method: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete to directly open the clearing window.

Mobile
  1. Open the Edge app.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) at the bottom.
  3. Tap "Settings".
  4. Tap "Privacy and security".
  5. Tap "Clear browsing data".
  6. Choose the time range and data types to clear.
  7. Tap "Clear data".
5. How to clear cache in Samsung Internet browser
Mobile
  1. Open the Samsung Internet app.
  2. Tap the menu button (≡) at the bottom-right corner.
  3. Tap "Settings".
  4. Tap "Privacy and security".
  5. Tap "Delete browsing data".
  6. Check "Cache" and "Cookies and site data".
  7. Tap the "Delete" button.

Telegram MiniApp Cache Clearing

6. How to clear MiniApp cache in Telegram PC
PC
  1. Open the Telegram desktop app.
  2. Click the hamburger menu (≡) in the top-left corner.
  3. Select "Settings".
  4. Click "Advanced".
  5. Click "Data and Storage".
  6. Click "Clear Cache".
  7. Confirm by clicking "Clear".

This will clear the cache for all MiniApps together. If you want to reset only a specific MiniApp, try reopening the MiniApp and pressing the refresh key (F5), or check if the MiniApp has cache clearing options in its settings.

7. How to clear MiniApp cache in Telegram Mobile
Android
  1. Open the Telegram app.
  2. Tap the hamburger menu (≡) in the top-left corner.
  3. Tap "Settings".
  4. Tap "Data and Storage".
  5. Tap "Storage Usage".
  6. Tap "Clear Cache".
  7. Confirm by tapping "Clear".
iOS
  1. Open the Telegram app.
  2. Tap "Settings" at the bottom.
  3. Tap "Data and Storage".
  4. Tap "Storage Usage".
  5. Tap "Clear Cache".
  6. Confirm by tapping "Clear Cache".

Completely closing and reopening the Telegram app can also help reset the cache for MiniApps. Additionally, some MiniApps may provide their own cache clearing options within the app, so check the MiniApp's settings.

8. How to refresh a specific web game/MiniApp

Ways to refresh a web game or MiniApp without completely clearing the cache:

PC
  • Most browsers: Press the F5 key or Ctrl + R.
  • Force refresh (bypass cache): Press Ctrl + F5 or Ctrl + Shift + R.
  • Telegram MiniApp: Press the F5 key while in the MiniApp, or close and reopen the MiniApp.
Mobile
  • Most browsers: Pull down on the screen to refresh.
  • Telegram MiniApp: Close and reopen the MiniApp, or completely exit the Telegram app and relaunch it.
9. What to do if problems persist after clearing cache

If issues persist after clearing the cache, try these methods:

  • Update your browser or Telegram app to the latest version.
  • Try accessing with a different browser.
  • Switch between mobile data and Wi-Fi.
  • Restart your device.
  • Contact the web game or MiniApp developer for support.

For some games or apps, login information may be stored on servers and won't be reset by clearing the cache alone. In such cases, try logging out and logging back in to your account.

10. Why regular cache management is necessary

Reasons why regular cache management is important:

  • Improved web game performance: Outdated cache files can slow down game loading times.
  • Bug fixes: New versions of updated games or apps may not load properly with old cache.
  • Free up storage space: Cache can take up significant storage space over time.
  • Data protection: Cache may contain personal information, so periodic deletion helps with security.

In most cases, it's good practice to clear your cache once every 1-3 months. Always try clearing the cache when you encounter issues with web games or MiniApps.
