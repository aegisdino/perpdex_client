USDT Transfer Guide FAQ

1. What is TronLink?

TronLink is a cryptocurrency wallet that allows you to send and receive USDT. It can be used as a Chrome browser extension or a mobile app.

2. How do I install TronLink?

For Chrome Browser:

  1. Access the Chrome Web Store by searching for "TronLink" or using this link.
  2. Click the "Add to Chrome" button to install the extension.
  3. After installation, the TronLink icon (red logo) will appear in the top right corner of your browser. Click it to launch.

For Mobile:

  • Download the app by searching for "TronLink" in Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS).
3. How do I create a new wallet?

First Launch:

  • When opening TronLink for the first time, you'll see options to "Create" a new wallet or "Restore" an existing one. Select "Create" to make a new wallet.

Setting a Password:

  • Enter and confirm a password with at least 8 characters. Remember this password as you'll need it to log into your wallet.

Backing Up Mnemonic Phrases:

  • 12 English words (mnemonic seed) will be displayed. These words are essential for wallet recovery, so keep them in a safe place in the correct order.
  • Never store them on a digital device.
  • Complete wallet creation by selecting the words in the correct order on the next screen.

Checking Wallet Address:

  • Your generated wallet address (a 34-character string starting with T) will be displayed. This address is used when receiving TRX or tokens.
4. How do I recover an existing wallet?
  • Select "Restore" and enter the 12 mnemonic words in the correct order from your previous backup.
  • Set a new password to complete the recovery of your existing wallet.
5. How do I check my assets?
  • View your TRX and other tokens (TRC-10, TRC-20, etc.) on the main screen of TronLink.
6. How do I receive coins?
  • Click the "Receive" button to copy your wallet address or generate a QR code.
  • Provide this address to exchanges or other users to receive TRX or tokens.
7. How do I send coins?
  1. Click the "Send" button.
  2. Enter the recipient's wallet address.
  3. Select the amount and type of token to send.
  4. Verify you have sufficient Bandwidth or Energy for the transaction. If insufficient, you'll need to charge with TRX or obtain resources via Freeze.
  5. Enter your password to complete the transfer.
8. Why are mnemonic words important?

Mnemonic words (12 English words) are the only way to recover your wallet. If you lose these words, you will lose access to all assets in your wallet. Keep them in a safe place.

Never store your mnemonic words on any digital device. Write them down on paper and keep them in a secure location.

9. What are Bandwidth and Energy?

These are resources needed for transactions on the Tron network:

  • Bandwidth: Used for regular token transfers.
  • Energy: Used when executing smart contracts.
  • You can obtain these resources by freezing TRX. If you don't have enough resources, a small amount of TRX will be consumed automatically during transactions.
10. What precautions should I take when using TronLink?
  • Keep your password and mnemonic words secure.
  • Double-check the recipient's address before sending. Coins sent to incorrect addresses cannot be recovered.
  • Only install TronLink through official app stores or web stores.
  • Don't connect your wallet to suspicious websites or apps.
  • Consider testing with small amounts before making significant transfers.
